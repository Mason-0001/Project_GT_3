About Tornadoes

Tornadoes are deadly natural disasters with extreme winds that can exceed 200 miles per hour. Every year, more than 1,200 tornadoes touch down in the United States alone. Tornadoes develop rapidly, giving little to no warning of when or where they will strike, and that's why we've developed these visualizations.

Tornado Data source

We are using data from the Storm Prediction Center of the NOAA's National Weather Service. Our data shows the actual tornadoes from 2007 to 2021.
